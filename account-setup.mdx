Setting up your Galagos account takes only a few minutes. This page covers registration, plan selection, account settings, and optional configuration such as connecting a custom LLM.

## Creating an account

Go to [galagos.ai/register](https://galagos.ai/register) and choose one of the following sign-up methods:

- **Email and password** — enter your email address, choose a password, and confirm your email via the link Galagos sends you.
- **Sign in with Google** — authenticate with your Google account for faster access without a separate password.

Once registered, you can log in at [galagos.ai/login](https://galagos.ai/login) at any time.

<Note>
  The free Individual plan is available during beta at no cost and does not require a credit card. You receive 20 one-time credits to run your first analyses.
</Note>

## Choosing a plan

After registration you are automatically placed on the free Individual plan. If your needs grow, you can upgrade from **Settings > Billing** at any time.

| Plan | Price | Credits/mo | Storage/project | Active projects |
|------|-------|-----------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Individual | Free (beta) | 20 one-time | 10 GB | 5 |
| Starter | €35/mo | 50 | 25 GB | 10 |
| Professional | €150/mo | 200 | 100 GB | 50 |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom | Custom | Unlimited |

For a full comparison of features across plans, see the [plans overview](/plans/overview).

## Account settings

Access your account settings by clicking your profile icon in the top-right corner and selecting **Settings**.

### Changing your email or password

Navigate to **Settings > Profile** to update your email address or change your password. If you signed up with Google, password management is handled through your Google account.

### Managing billing

Go to **Settings > Billing** to:

- View your current plan and credit balance
- Upgrade or downgrade your subscription
- Update your payment method
- Download past invoices

### Deleting your account

To delete your account and all associated data, contact [support](/support/contact). Galagos will permanently remove your data in accordance with its GDPR data deletion obligations.

## Understanding credits

Credits are the unit Galagos uses to measure LLM usage. Each analysis consumes a number of credits proportional to the complexity and length of the workflow. Credits are deducted when an analysis runs, not when you upload files or browse results.

<Info>
  The free Individual plan's 20 credits are one-time and do not renew. Paid plans receive a fresh credit allocation each billing month.
</Info>

For a detailed breakdown of how credits are calculated and what consumes storage, see [Credits and storage](/concepts/credits-and-storage).

## Connecting a custom LLM

On the **Starter plan and above**, you can connect your own LLM API key so Galagos routes analysis requests through a model of your choice. This is useful if your organization has negotiated enterprise pricing with an AI provider or has data residency requirements that mandate a specific deployment.

<Tip>
  Using a custom LLM does not change how Galagos plans or executes workflows — it only replaces the underlying model that powers the natural language interface.
</Tip>

To configure a custom LLM, go to **Settings > LLM configuration**. For full setup instructions, see [Bring your own LLM](/security/bring-your-own-llm).
