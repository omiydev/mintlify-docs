The Enterprise plan is designed for research labs, biotech companies, and academic institutions that need more than a single-user license. If your team shares data across multiple researchers, runs large-scale genomics pipelines, or operates under strict compliance requirements, Enterprise gives you the infrastructure and support to do that securely and at scale.

## What's included

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Multi-user shared workspaces" icon="users">
    Multiple researchers can collaborate inside shared projects — uploading files, reviewing workflows, and accessing results — without needing separate accounts or manual file transfers.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pooled compute credits & custom storage" icon="layer-group">
    Credits and storage are pooled across your team rather than siloed per user, so heavy users and light users naturally balance each other out.
  </Card>
  <Card title="HPC compute resources" icon="server">
    Large-scale analyses — whole-genome sequencing, bulk RNA-seq cohorts, protein structure prediction — can be offloaded to high-performance compute nodes, avoiding the bottlenecks of standard cloud VMs.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Dedicated infrastructure & SSO" icon="shield-halved">
    Your team's workloads run on dedicated infrastructure, isolated from other Galagos tenants. Single sign-on (SSO) integrates with your existing identity provider for centralised access control.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Custom pipeline integrations" icon="plug">
    Connect Galagos to your existing LIMS, data lake, or internal tooling. Our team works with you to build and maintain the integrations your workflows depend on.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Dedicated success manager & priority support" icon="headset">
    You get a named success manager who understands your research context, plus priority response times so blocking issues are resolved quickly.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  On-premise deployment is available for organisations that cannot send data to cloud infrastructure. Speak to us during the evaluation call to discuss requirements.
</Note>

## Pricing

Enterprise pricing is custom and depends on your team size, compute requirements, storage needs, and compliance obligations. There is no publicly listed price — contact us to receive a tailored quote.

## How to get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get in touch">
    Email us at [contact@galagos.ai](mailto:contact@galagos.ai) with a brief description of your team size and the types of analyses you need to run. We typically respond within one business day.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Book an evaluation call">
    Schedule a 30-minute call with our team to walk through your requirements, discuss infrastructure options, and ask any questions about security or compliance.

    [Book a meeting](https://calendar.app.google/DFVrB9FAS9db65oEA)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive a custom proposal">
    We'll put together a proposal covering pricing, infrastructure setup, SLA terms, and an onboarding timeline tailored to your organisation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Onboard your team">
    Once agreed, your dedicated success manager will guide you through workspace setup, SSO configuration, and any custom pipeline integrations.
  </Step>
</Steps>
