Galagos offers four self-service plans plus a custom Enterprise option for labs and organisations that need multi-user workspaces or HPC compute. All plans include AI-powered bioinformatics analysis, multi-agent research workflows, file uploads, and visualization. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time, and no contract is required.

<Note>
  The Advanced plan is the most popular choice for researchers who run analyses regularly. It offers the best balance of credits, storage, and cost.
</Note>

## How credits work

Galagos uses credits to meter LLM inference during analysis. Each time the AI reasons about your data — planning a workflow, interpreting results, or generating a report — it consumes one or more credits. More complex, multi-step analyses use more credits than simple single-tool runs.

Credits reset on your monthly billing date. Unused credits do not roll over to the next month.

<Tip>
  If you regularly hit your credit limit, upgrading one tier typically doubles your monthly allowance. You can also connect your own LLM on Starter and above to reduce credit consumption.
</Tip>

## Individual plan (free during beta)

The Individual plan is free while Galagos is in beta — no credit card required. You receive 20 one-time credits to explore the platform and run your first analyses. Once those credits are used, you can upgrade to a paid plan to continue.

<Info>
  Beta access may end without notice. When the beta period closes, Individual plan users will be invited to choose a paid plan.
</Info>

## Changing your plan

You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from **Settings → Billing** in your account. Changes take effect immediately for upgrades; downgrades apply at the end of your current billing period.

## Payment methods

Galagos accepts credit and debit cards, as well as Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Klarna at checkout.

## Enterprise and team use

If you are working in a research lab, biotech company, or academic institution and need shared workspaces, pooled compute, or enterprise security (SSO), the Enterprise plan is built for you.
