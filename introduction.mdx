Galagos is an AI-powered bioinformatics platform built for research teams who need rigorous analysis without the bottleneck of scarce bioinformatics expertise. You describe your research question in plain English, upload your data, and Galagos plans and executes the full pipeline — from raw files to publication-ready reports — entirely on your behalf.

## The problem Galagos solves

Modern biology generates data faster than most teams can analyze it. Sequencing costs have dropped dramatically, but the pool of skilled bioinformaticians has not grown at the same pace. The result: valuable datasets wait weeks for analysis, and when analysis does happen it is often a one-off script that is difficult to audit, reproduce, or hand off to a collaborator.

Galagos is designed for:

- **Biomedical researchers** who want to explore their own data without depending on a bioinformatics core
- **Bioinformatics teams** who want to accelerate throughput without scaling headcount
- **R&D leaders** who need reproducible, auditable analyses across multiple projects and collaborators

## How Galagos helps

**Natural language interface.** You describe what you need — "Perform differential gene expression analysis comparing treatment vs control groups" — and Galagos selects the right tools, writes and runs the code, and returns structured results. No scripting required.

**Autonomous pipelines.** Galagos handles multi-stage workflows end-to-end. If a step fails, it detects the error and tries an alternative approach automatically, without interrupting you for every decision.

**250+ pre-built Skills.** AlphaFold, Scanpy, AutoDock Vina, GATK, ColabFold, IQ-TREE, pydeseq2, RDKit, ChEMBL, and hundreds more are ready to use. Toggle the ones a project needs, or capture your own preferred protocols as custom Skills the rest of your team can reuse.

**Reproducible outputs.** Every workflow preserves its context, steps, inputs, and outputs in a single project. Anyone who needs to verify or repeat the analysis has everything they need in one place.

**Literature-grounded insights.** Galagos pulls supporting scientific context from PubMed, UniProt, and NCBI so your reports connect results to the published evidence.

## Platform overview

Galagos organizes your work into three building blocks:

- **Projects** — a persistent workspace that holds your files, analyses, and outputs. Storage ranges from 10 GB on the free plan to 100 GB on Professional.
- **Skills** — pre-built or custom analysis capabilities that Galagos draws on when planning a workflow. Browse the full library or create your own.
- **Credits** — the unit of LLM usage consumed per analysis. The free Individual plan includes 20 one-time credits; paid plans replenish credits monthly.
