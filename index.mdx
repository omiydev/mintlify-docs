## How Galagos works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload your data">
    Drag and drop your files — FASTQ, VCF, BAM, H5AD, CSV, and more are all supported natively.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe your question">
    Type your research question in plain English. Galagos plans the workflow, selects the right tools, and runs the analysis end-to-end.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your results">
    Get structured reports, visualizations (volcano plots, heatmaps, bar charts), and literature-grounded insights — all in one workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share and reproduce">
    Every workflow preserves its context, steps, and outputs so your team can review, repeat, and defend the result.
  </Step>
</Steps>
